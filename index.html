A stroke is a medical emergency. Symptoms of stroke include trouble walking, speaking and understanding, as well as paralysis or numbness of the face, arm or leg. Early treatment with medication like tPA (clot buster) can minimize brain damage. Other treatments focus on limiting complications and preventing additional strokes.

A brain stroke is a medical condition which which occurs when the blood supply to a part of the brain is blocked or a blood vessel in the brain bursts .
This project aims to deliver a user-friendly website which assists people to accurately predict if there is a possibity of having a brain stroke or not . so that they can approach a medical proffessional timely

Brain stroke Symptoms

  • Sudden numbness or weakness of the face, arm or leg,
  • especially on one side of the eyes.
  • Sudden confusion, trouble speaking or understanding.
  • Sudden trouble seeing in one or both eyes.
  • Sudden trouble walking, dizziness, loss of balance and coordination.
  • Sudden severe headache with no known cause.

What causes a brain stroke

  • obesity

  • high blood pressure
  • diabetes
  • high cholesterol

  • Sedentary lifestyle
  • Excessive consumption of alcohol
  • Smoking
