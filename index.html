A brain stroke is a medical condition which which occurs when the blood supply to a part of the brain is blocked or a blood vessel in the brain bursts . This is a third party website that enables people to determine whether there is a chance of brain stroke or not from the comfort of their home and consult doctors accordingly

Brain stroke Symptoms

Sudden numbness or weakness of the face, arm or leg, especially on one side of the eyes.

Sudden confusion, trouble speaking or understanding.

Sudden trouble seeing in one or both eyes.

Sudden trouble walking, dizziness, loss of balance and coordination.

Sudden severe headache with no known cause.
